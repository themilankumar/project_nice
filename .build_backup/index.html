Who We Are

Born to Race,
Built to Win

CU Motorsports is a student-driven Formula Bharat racing team from Chandigarh University. We bring together the brightest minds in engineering, design, and management to conceive, design, fabricate, and race a formula-style vehicle at the prestigious Formula Bharat competition.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of automotive engineering while providing hands-on experience to future engineers. Every component, from the chassis to the aerodynamics package, is designed and built by our team members right here on campus.

Our Divisions

Team Structure

A well-oiled machine — both on track and off. Meet the departments that make it all happen.

🏗️

Chassis & Frame

Design and fabrication of the tubular space frame chassis, ensuring structural rigidity, driver safety, and optimal weight distribution.

🔩

Powertrain

Engine tuning, intake & exhaust design, drivetrain optimization, and fuel system calibration for peak performance on track.

🌊

Aerodynamics

CFD simulations, wind tunnel testing, and aero package design — front wing, rear wing, undertray, and sidepods for maximum downforce.

🔄

Suspension & Dynamics

Suspension geometry, damper tuning, steering system, and brake design for precise vehicle dynamics and driver confidence.

⚡

Electronics & Data

ECU mapping, wiring harness, telemetry, data acquisition, and dashboard systems that keep the driver informed and the car optimized.

📢

Business & Marketing

Sponsorship management, brand identity, cost reporting, and business presentations — the backbone of our operations off the track.

Our Journey

Road to Formula Bharat

From concept to competition — every milestone on our road to the track.

Phase 1 — Jun 2025

Team Formation

Recruitment of core team members across engineering and management departments at Chandigarh University.

Phase 2 — Aug 2025

Concept Design

Initial vehicle concept, rulebook study, suspension geometry, and aerodynamic simulations finalized.

Phase 3 — Nov 2025

Detailed Design & FEA

Complete CAD modeling, finite element analysis, and design reviews across all sub-systems.

Phase 4 — Jan 2026

Manufacturing & Assembly

Chassis fabrication, component machining, wiring harness, and vehicle assembly in the university workshop.

Phase 5 — Mar 2026

Testing & Validation

Shakedown runs, brake tests, driver training, and data-driven setup refinements.

Phase 6 — Jul 2026

Formula Bharat Competition

The big stage — static events, dynamic events, and endurance. Let's race! 🏁
