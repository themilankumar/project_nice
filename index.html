Who We Are

Born to Race,
Built to Win

CU Motorsports is a student-driven Formula Bharat racing team from Chandigarh University. We bring together the brightest minds in engineering, design, and management to conceive, design, fabricate, and race a formula-style vehicle at the prestigious Formula Bharat competition.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of automotive engineering while providing hands-on experience to future engineers. Every component, from the chassis to the aerodynamics package, is designed and built by our team members right here on campus.
